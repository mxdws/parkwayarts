Craft, Create, and Connect

Discover the art of hand built ceramics, mosaics, silver clay, and more at Parkway Arts community pottery and craft studio, launching in Early 2025!

Nestled in the heart of Didcot, opposite the station, Parkway Arts community studio is a new pottery and craft studio designed to inspire creativity, nurture mindfulness, and connect like-minded individuals.Whether you’re a complete beginner or an experienced maker, our welcoming space offers:

  • Hand-Built Ceramics: Learn the art of shaping clay by hand

  • Mosaic Making: Craft intricate designs using colorful tiles

  • Silver Clay Creations: Create stunning, wearable art

… and more exciting crafts coming soon!We’re dedicated to making craft accessible for all, with workshops, memberships, and drop-in sessions designed to fit your lifestyle.
